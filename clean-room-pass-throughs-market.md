<p><p>Clean room pass throughs are used in industries such as pharmaceuticals and electronics to maintain a controlled environment and prevent contamination. There are two main types of pass throughs: wall-mounted and floor-mounted. Wall-mounted pass throughs are fixed to the clean room walls, allowing for easy transfer of materials between rooms. Floor-mounted pass throughs, on the other hand, are installed flush with the clean room floor, enabling the transfer of larger equipment or materials on carts. Both types contribute to the efficiency and cleanliness of clean room operations.</p></p>
