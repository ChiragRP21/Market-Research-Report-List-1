<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Boat spinnaker market consists of two main types: symmetric spinnakers and asymmetric spinnakers. Symmetric spinnakers are designed for downwind sailing, and are identical on both sides with the bowline attached to the bowsprit or mast. They require a crew to handle the various control lines. Asymmetric spinnakers, on the other hand, are easier to handle single-handedly as they are fitted with a curved luff allowing for off-centerline sailing. They are attached to the bow or a retractable pole. Both types cater to different sailing needs and preferences.</p></p>
