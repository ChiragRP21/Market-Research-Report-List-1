They provide a path for fault currents to flow, ensuring system stability and protecting equipment. Earthing transformers are available in two market types: oil-type and dry-type. Oil-type earthing transformers are filled with insulating oil and are typically used in outdoor installations. On the other hand, dry-type earthing transformers use solid insulation materials and are commonly employed in indoor installations where fire safety is a concern.</p></p>
