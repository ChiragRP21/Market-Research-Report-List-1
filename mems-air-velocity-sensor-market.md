<p><p>The MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) Air Velocity Sensor market comprises two main types: Mechanical Air Velocity Sensor and Ultrasonic Air Velocity Sensor. Mechanical sensors measure air velocity using mechanical components such as wind vanes or anemometers, providing accurate readings but are susceptible to wear and require periodic maintenance. On the other hand, Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to measure air velocity, offering non-contact measurement, durability, and minimal maintenance. These sensors find applications in various industries, including HVAC systems, weather monitoring, and automotive air flow control.</p></p>
