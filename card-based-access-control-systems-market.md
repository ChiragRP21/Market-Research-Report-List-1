<p><p>Card Based Access Control Systems are used for security purposes in various industries. The market is divided into two types: Card Reader With Button and Card Reader Without Button. </p><p>In the Card Reader With Button system, users need to scan their access card and press a button to gain entry. This additional step adds an extra layer of security as it prevents unauthorized individuals from gaining access. </p><p>On the other hand, the Card Reader Without Button system allows users to simply scan their access card without the need to press a button. This type of system is more convenient as it eliminates the additional step, making it quicker for authorized personnel to enter restricted areas.</p></p>
