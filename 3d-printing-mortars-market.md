<p><p>The 3D printing mortars market consists of two main types: fiber-reinforced mortar and polymer-modified cementitious mortar. Fiber-reinforced mortar incorporates fibers such as glass or carbon to enhance its mechanical properties and durability. This type of mortar is commonly used in applications that require high strength and resistance, such as construction. On the other hand, polymer-modified cementitious mortar comprises polymers to enhance its workability and adhesive properties. This type of mortar is widely used in various construction projects due to its improved performance and flexibility. Both types play a crucial role in the rapidly growing field of 3D printing in the construction industry.</p></p>
