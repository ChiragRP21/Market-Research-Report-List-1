<p><p>The athrectomy devices market consists of several types: directional atherectomy, rotational atherectomy, orbital atherectomy, and laser atherectomy. Directional atherectomy involves removing plaque from arteries using a cutting device inserted through a catheter. Rotational atherectomy involves using a high-speed rotational burr to grind plaque away. Orbital atherectomy uses a diamond-coated crown that orbits inside the artery, sanding away plaque. Laser atherectomy utilizes laser energy to vaporize or break down plaque. These different types of athrectomy devices provide physicians with various options for treating arterial blockages.</p></p>
