<p><p>The automotive thermoplastic elastomer market is divided into four major types: styrene block copolymers (SBS, SEBS), thermoplastic poliolefin (TPO), thermoplastic polyurethane (TPU), and others. Styrene block copolymers are versatile and offer excellent elasticity, making them suitable for various automotive applications. Thermoplastic poliolefin offers a balance between performance and cost-effectiveness, making it a popular choice in automotive manufacturing. Thermoplastic polyurethane is known for its high strength and durability, providing resistance to abrasion, chemicals, and UV radiation. The "others" category encompasses other types of automotive thermoplastic elastomers that may offer unique properties or fulfill specific requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
