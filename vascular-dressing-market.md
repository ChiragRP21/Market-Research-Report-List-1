<p><p>Vascular dressings refer to specialized wound dressings that are designed to manage and promote healing in vascular wounds. The market for vascular dressings can be categorized into two major types: antimicrobial vascular dressings and non-antimicrobial vascular dressings. </p><p>Antimicrobial vascular dressings are infused with agents that have the ability to kill or inhibit the growth of microorganisms. These dressings help prevent infections in vascular wounds and promote faster healing. </p><p>On the other hand, non-antimicrobial vascular dressings do not have any anti-microbial properties. They are primarily used to provide a barrier between the wound and external factors, such as dirt and debris, and to maintain a moist environment to aid in healing. These dressings are commonly used in wounds with a low risk of infection.</p></p>
