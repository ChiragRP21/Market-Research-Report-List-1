<p><strong>The Military Phased Array Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AESA</li><li>PESA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Military Phased Array Antenna Market comprises two main types: AESA (Active Electronically Scanned Array) and PESA (Passive Electronically Scanned Array). AESA systems use individual transmit-receive modules, enabling rapid beamforming and electronic steering of the antenna. They ensure higher reliability, improved performance, and flexible operations. PESA systems, on the other hand, employ a single high-power transmitter and multiple passive elements for beamforming. While PESA has lower costs, AESA offers advanced features. Both systems play crucial roles in military applications, such as advanced radar systems, securing communication networks, and enhancing command and control capabilities.</p></p>
