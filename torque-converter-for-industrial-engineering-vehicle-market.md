<p><p>In the industrial and engineering vehicle market, there are two types of torque converters: single-stage torque converters and multistage torque converters. </p><p>A single-stage torque converter is a type of fluid coupling that transfers torque from the engine to the transmission in a single stage, without any additional gears. It is simpler in design and more compact, making it suitable for applications where space is limited. </p><p>On the other hand, a multistage torque converter is a more complex system that includes multiple stages of gears to increase torque multiplication. This allows for greater efficiency and higher torque output, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications where more power is required.</p></p>
