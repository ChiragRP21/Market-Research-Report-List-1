It is used to treat children with SAM and has been proven to be highly effective in community-based treatment programs. RUSF, on the other hand, is a food product that is used as a supplementary source of nutrition for children with MAM. It is typically made from locally available ingredients such as grains, pulses, fortified vegetable oil, and micronutrient powders.</p><p>The future outlook of the RUTF and RUSF market is promising.
