<p><strong>The Concrete Waterproofing Admixture Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crystalline Waterproofing Admixture</li><li>Pore-blocking Waterproofing Admixture</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concrete waterproofing admixture market types refer to different additives that are used to enhance the waterproofing properties of concrete. Crystalline waterproofing admixture creates a crystalline structure inside the concrete, which blocks water penetration. Pore-blocking waterproofing admixture fills the capillary pores in the concrete, preventing the passage of water. Other types of admixtures include hydrophobic and superhydrophobic admixtures, which create a water-repellent surface, and cementitious waterproofing admixtures, which increase the density and impermeability of the concrete. These admixtures are designed to improve the durability and longevity of concrete structures in various applications.</p></p>
