<p><strong>The Food Grade Grippers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Grippers</li><li>Robohand Grippers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Food Grade Grippers market includes two main types: Pneumatic Grippers and Robohand Grippers. Pneumatic Grippers are air-powered devices used for gripping and handling food-grade products. They rely on compressed air to create the required force to hold onto objects securely. On the other hand, Robohand Grippers are specifically designed robotic grippers that meet the necessary hygiene standards for handling food. These grippers provide precise and efficient handling of food items, ensuring cleanliness and preventing contamination. Both types play a crucial role in the food industry by facilitating safe and efficient food handling processes.</p></p>
