<p><p>The Automotive Evaporative Emission Control (EVAP) System market consists of various components that help in reducing fuel evaporation and emission of harmful vapors from vehicles. These components include fuel tanks, gas caps, liquid vapor separators, canisters, control valves, sensors, and hoses. The fuel tank stores the fuel, while the gas cap seals the tank to prevent vapor leakage. The liquid vapor separator separates the fuel from the vapor. Canisters absorb and store the fuel vapors, control valves regulate the flow, sensors monitor the system, and hoses connect these components for the complete EVAP system to function effectively.</p></p>
