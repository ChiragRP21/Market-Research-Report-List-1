<p><p>The home non-invasive ventilator market for COPD consists of two main types: single level ventilators and bilevel ventilators. Single level ventilators deliver a constant pressure throughout the breathing cycle, helping patients with COPD to maintain their lung function. On the other hand, bilevel ventilators provide a higher pressure during inhalation and a lower pressure during exhalation, allowing for more customized treatment for patients with COPD. Both types of ventilators are designed for home use, providing respiratory support for individuals with COPD in the comfort of their own homes.</p></p>
