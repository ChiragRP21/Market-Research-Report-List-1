<p><p>The Ground Support Equipment (GSE) market consists of two types of equipment: Powered Equipment and Non-Powered Equipment. Powered Equipment refers to machinery that relies on an external power source, such as electric or fuel-powered systems, to perform tasks like towing, cargo loading, or powering aircraft for maintenance purposes. On the other hand, Non-Powered Equipment does not require any external power source and includes items like dollies, ramps, or chocks used for baggage handling, aircraft cleaning, or aircraft servicing. Both types of equipment are crucial for efficient aircraft operations on the ground.</p></p>
