<p><p>IC test sockets are essential tools used in the semiconductor industry for testing and validating integrated circuits (ICs). There are several types of IC test sockets available in the market. BGA (Ball Grid Array) sockets are designed for ICs with a grid of solder balls on their underside. LGA (Land Grid Array) sockets are used for ICs with lands or pads instead of solder balls. QFN (Quad Flat No-Lead) sockets are suitable for ICs with a flat package and no leads. Besides these, there are other socket variations catering to specific IC packaging requirements.</p></p>
