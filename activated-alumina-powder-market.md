<p><p>Activated alumina powder is available in various mesh sizes, including 80-150 mesh, 150-300 mesh, and above 300 mesh. The mesh size refers to the number of openings per inch within the sieve used for grading the powder. The 80-150 mesh powder has particles sized between 80 to 150 mesh, while the 150-300 mesh powder consists of particles ranging from 150 to 300 mesh. Lastly, above 300 mesh powder contains particles smaller than 300 mesh. The different mesh sizes enable the selection of activated alumina powder depending on specific application requirements such as desired surface area, adsorptive capacity, or pore size distribution.</p></p>
