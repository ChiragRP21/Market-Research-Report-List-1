The CR Tech Digital X-Ray System uses computed radiography technology to capture X-ray images, which are then digitized for analysis. On the other hand, the DR Tech Digital X-Ray System utilizes direct radiography technology, where the X-ray images are captured directly using a digital detector. Both systems offer advancements in image quality, workflow efficiency, and radiation dose reduction for pediatric patients.</p></p>
