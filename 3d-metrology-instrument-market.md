<p><ul><li>Coordinate Measuring Machine (CMM)</li><li>Optical Digitizer and Scanner (ODS)</li><li>Video Measuring Machine (VMM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Metrology Instrument Market includes three main types of instruments: Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMM), Optical Digitizers and Scanners (ODS), and Video Measuring Machines (VMM). CMMs use touch probes to measure physical objects accurately, making them ideal for dimensional inspection and quality control. ODS instruments use lasers or non-contact methods to capture object data and recreate it digitally. VMMs combine video technologies with advanced software to measure the dimensions of objects quickly and accurately. These instruments find applications in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
