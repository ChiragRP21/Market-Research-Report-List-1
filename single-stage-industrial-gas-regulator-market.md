Membrane Type regulators use a flexible membrane to control gas flow, ensuring precise pressure regulation. Spring Type regulators use a spring-loaded diaphragm to adjust gas pressure. Piston Type regulators use a movable piston to regulate pressure, offering stable control in high flow applications. Plug Type regulators utilize a tapered plug to control gas flow, ensuring accurate pressure adjustment. Each type offers unique features and benefits according to specific industry requirements.</p></p>
