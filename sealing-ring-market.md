<p><p>The Sealing Ring market can be classified into various types, including NBR, HNBR, SIL, VITON, FLS, and others. NBR, also known as nitrile rubber, offers excellent resistance to oil and fuels, making it suitable for applications in the automotive industry. HNBR, or hydrogenated nitrile rubber, provides improved heat resistance and broader chemical compatibility. SIL, or silicone rubber, is known for its high-temperature resistance and excellent electrical insulation properties. VITON, a fluoroelastomer, exhibits exceptional resistance to chemicals and high temperatures. FLS refers to fluorsilicone rubber, which combines fluoroelastomer and silicone properties. Other types may include EPDM, PTFE, and natural rubber, among others.</p></p>
