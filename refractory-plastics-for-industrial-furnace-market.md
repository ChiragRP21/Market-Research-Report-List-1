<p><p>Refractory plastics for the industrial furnace market are categorized into two types: plastic mouldable refractories and high alumina ramming mixes. Plastic mouldable refractories are materials that can be easily molded to fit the necessary shape within the furnace. On the other hand, high alumina ramming mixes consist of a mixture of high alumina aggregates and binders, which are used for ramming applications in the furnace lining. Both types are essential in industrial furnaces to provide insulation and protect the furnace structure from high temperatures and chemical reactions.</p></p>
