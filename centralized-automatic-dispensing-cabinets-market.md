Stationary Dispensing Cabinets are fixed in one location and are typically used in pharmacy settings or medication storage areas. Mobile Dispensing Cabinets, on the other hand, are equipped with wheels and can be easily moved around the facility, allowing healthcare providers to access medications directly at the patient's bedside.
