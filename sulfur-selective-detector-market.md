<p><ul><li>Sulfur Chemiluminescence Detector（SCD）</li><li>Flame Photometric Detector (FPD)</li><li>Mass Spectrometry Detector (MSD)</li><li>Atomic Emission Detection (AED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sulfur selective detector market encompasses various types of detectors used for analyzing sulfur compounds. The Sulfur Chemiluminescence Detector (SCD) relies on the chemical reaction of sulfur compounds to generate light for detection. The Flame Photometric Detector (FPD) uses a flame to ionize sulfur compounds and measures the emitted light. The Mass Spectrometry Detector (MSD) identifies sulfur compounds based on their unique mass-to-charge ratio. The Atomic Emission Detection (AED) utilizes the emission of light from excited sulfur atoms for detection purposes.</p></p>
