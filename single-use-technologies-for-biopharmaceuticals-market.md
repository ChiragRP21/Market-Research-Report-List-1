Media bags are sterile containers used for storing and transporting liquids used in bioprocessing. Bioprocess containers are flexible bags used for the sterile containment of liquids, offering the advantages of mobility and easy disposal. Other single-use technologies include sensors, connectors, and tubing sets that facilitate bioprocessing operations.</p></p>
