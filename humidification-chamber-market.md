<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The humidification chamber market consists of two main types: auto-fill chambers and manual-fill chambers. Auto-fill chambers are designed to automatically fill with water when the water level is low, ensuring a constant supply of humidity. On the other hand, manual-fill chambers require manual intervention to add water when the humidity levels drop. Both types serve the purpose of maintaining optimal humidity levels in environments such as hospitals or restaurants, but their difference lies in the method of water replenishment.</p></p>
