<p><ul><li>Pressure Type</li><li>Gravity Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A hydroseparator is a device used to separate liquids of different densities in various industries. There are two main types of hydroseparators: pressure type and gravity type. </p><p>In the pressure type market, hydroseparators are designed to create a pressure differential to separate the liquids. This is achieved by using pumps or compressed air to create high pressure, forcing the heavier liquid to separate from the lighter one.</p><p>In the gravity type market, hydroseparators rely on gravity to separate the liquids. The system is designed in a way that allows the heavier liquid to settle at the bottom, while the lighter liquid stays on top.</p><p>Both types of hydroseparators serve the purpose of efficient liquid separation, but their operation mechanisms differ.</p></p>
