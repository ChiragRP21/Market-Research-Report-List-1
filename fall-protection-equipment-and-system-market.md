<p><p>Fall protection equipment and systems are essential safety measures designed to prevent accidents and injuries in industries where working at heights is common. The market for fall protection equipment includes various types such as harnesses, lanyards, self-retracting lifelines, belts, and other related products. Harnesses are wearable devices that distribute the force of a fall across the body, while lanyards are flexible lines that connect the harness to an anchor point. Self-retracting lifelines serve as safety ropes, automatically retracting during normal movement. Belts provide support and stability, and other fall protection equipment may include carabiners, hooks, and connectors.</p></p>
