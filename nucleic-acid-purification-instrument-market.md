<p><p>The nucleic acid purification instrument market consists of two types: fully automated and semi-automated. Fully automated instruments are designed to perform the entire purification process without any human intervention. They include features like automated sample loading, reagent dispensing, and purification protocol execution, providing high-throughput and standardized results. On the other hand, semi-automated instruments require some manual steps, such as sample loading or reagent addition, but still offer automation for crucial purification steps. Both types play a crucial role in nucleic acid research and diagnostics, offering different levels of automation to meet varying laboratory needs.</p></p>
