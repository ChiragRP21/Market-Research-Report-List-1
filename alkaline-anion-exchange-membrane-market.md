<p><p>The Alkaline Anion Exchange Membrane (AAEM) market consists of three major types: Perfluorocarboxylic acid membrane (Rf-COOH), Perfluorosulfonic acid membrane (Rf-SO3H), and Perfluorosulfonic acid/acid composite membrane (Rf-SO3H / Rf-COOH). Perfluorocarboxylic acid membrane is an AAEM made of perfluorocarboxylic acid as the main component. Perfluorosulfonic acid membrane is composed mainly of perfluorosulfonic acid. The Perfluorosulfonic acid/acid composite membrane is a combination of perfluorosulfonic acid and perfluorocarboxylic acid. These different types of AAEM are used in various applications and industries such as fuel cells, water electrolysis, and ion exchange membranes for energy storage and conversion.</p></p>
