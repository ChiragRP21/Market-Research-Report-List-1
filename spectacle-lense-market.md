<p><p>The spectacle lens market can be primarily classified into two main types: single vision spectacle lenses and progressive lenses. Single vision lenses are used to correct vision problems such as nearsightedness or farsightedness, providing clear vision at a specific distance. On the other hand, progressive lenses are multifocal lenses that offer seamless vision correction at various distances, eliminating the need for multiple pairs of glasses. These lenses are designed with a gradient of lens power, providing a smooth transition from near to intermediate to far distances.</p></p>
