<p><p>Alkali Silica Reactivity (AS) inhibitors are chemical compounds that are added to concrete to prevent or mitigate the damaging effects of alkali-silica reaction (ASR). ASR is a chemical reaction that occurs between the alkalis in cement and certain types of silica minerals present in aggregates used in concrete, leading to the formation of a gel that can expand and cause cracking, weakening the concrete structure over time.</p><p>The use of AS inhibitors in concrete helps to minimize or eliminate the expansion and cracking caused by ASR, thereby improving the durability and service life of concrete structures. These inhibitors work by chemically reacting with the alkalis in cement to form a non-expansive gel, preventing the harmful ASR process from occurring.</p><p>The global alkali silica reactivity inhibitors market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period.
