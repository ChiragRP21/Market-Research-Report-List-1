<p><strong>The Evaporation Coating Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PVD</li><li>CVD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Evaporation Coating Machine market is divided into two types - Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) and Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD). PVD uses a physical process to evaporate a source material and create a thin film coating on a product's surface. It is commonly used in industries like electronics and automotive. On the other hand, CVD involves a chemical reaction to deposit coating materials. It is widely used in semiconductor and optical industries for high-precision coating applications. Both PVD and CVD processes are essential in enhancing product performance, durability, and aesthetics.</p></p>
