<p><p>Special epoxy resin for wind turbine blades is a type of epoxy resin that is specifically designed for use in wind turbine blades. There are three main types of epoxy resin used in this market: hand paste resin, perfusion resin, and epoxy structural adhesive. Hand paste resin is a thick paste that is applied manually to repair small damages on the surface of the blade. Perfusion resin is a liquid resin that is injected into the inner layers of the blade to reinforce its structure. Epoxy structural adhesive is a strong adhesive that is used to bond different parts of the blade together.</p></p>
