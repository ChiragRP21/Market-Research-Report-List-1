<p><p>Agricultural pheromones are used in the farming industry to control and manage pest populations. There are two types of agricultural pheromone markets: sex pheromones and aggregation pheromones. Sex pheromones are chemicals released by female insects to attract males for mating. By using synthetic sex pheromones, farmers can disrupt insect reproduction and population growth. Aggregation pheromones, on the other hand, are released by insects to attract others of the same species. By using synthetic aggregation pheromones, farmers can lure insects into traps or away from crops, reducing damage caused by infestations.</p></p>
