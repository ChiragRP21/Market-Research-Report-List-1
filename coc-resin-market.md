<p><p>The COC (Cyclic Olefin Copolymer) resin market mainly consists of two types - ROMP (Ring-Opening Metathesis Polymerization) process and mCOC (Modified Cyclic Olefin Copolymer) process. In the ROMP process, COC resins are produced through a chemical reaction that involves the opening of rings in cyclic olefins. On the other hand, the mCOC process involves the modification of COC resins by incorporating other additives or altering the molecular structure. Both processes play a crucial role in catering to the growing demand for COC resin in various industries like packaging, medical, and electronics.</p></p>
