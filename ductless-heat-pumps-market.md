<p><p>Ductless heat pumps, also known as mini-split systems, are heating and cooling devices that do not require any ductwork. Ductless heat pumps provide a flexible and energy-efficient solution for heating and cooling individual rooms or zones in a building.</p><p>The ductless heat pumps market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years, owing to several factors. One of the key drivers is the increasing demand for energy-efficient HVAC systems, as ductless heat pumps are known for their higher energy efficiency compared to traditional systems. <p><p>The ductless heat pumps market can be categorized into single-zone and multi-zone systems. Single-zone heat pumps provide heating and cooling to a single room or area. They are ideal for smaller spaces or rooms with specific temperature needs. On the other hand, multi-zone heat pumps can provide heating and cooling to multiple rooms or areas independently. They offer flexibility and control, allowing different temperatures to be set in different zones. This makes them suitable for larger spaces or homes with varying climate preferences.</p></p>
