<p><p>The automotive gear shifter market consists of two main types: mechanical gear shifters and electronic gear shifters. Mechanical gear shifters are the traditional type, operated manually by the driver using a physical lever, cables, and linkages to engage different gears in the transmission. On the other hand, electronic gear shifters are more modern and utilize electronic sensors, actuators, and control units to change gears automatically or with the push of a button. Both types serve the same purpose of controlling the transmission, but they differ in their mechanisms and operation.</p></p>
