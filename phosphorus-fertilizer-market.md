<p><p>The phosphorus fertilizer market consists of various types of fertilizers. Diammonium Phosphate (DAP) fertilizer is a popular variant containing high levels of nitrogen and phosphorus. Monoammonium Phosphate (MAP) fertilizer also contains nitrogen and phosphorus, but in different proportions. NPKs fertilizer is a combination of nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium in varying ratios. Single Super Phosphate (SSP) fertilizer is made from sulfuric acid and ground phosphate rock. Lastly, the "Others" category includes different types of phosphorus fertilizers like Triple Super Phosphate (TSP), phosphoric acid, etc.</p></p>
