<p><p>Tabletop centrifuge market is categorized into low speed and high speed centrifuges. Low speed centrifuges are designed for applications that require gentle sample processing such as cell culture and blood separation. They operate at relatively low speeds and have a lower g-force, which minimizes the risk of damaging fragile samples. On the other hand, high speed centrifuges are used for applications that require faster and more efficient separations, such as DNA extraction and protein purification. They operate at higher speeds and generate a higher g-force, allowing for rapid separation of heavier particles from the sample.</p></p>
