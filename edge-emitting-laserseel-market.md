DFB lasers are known for their single-frequency operation and high wavelength stability. DBR lasers utilize a grating structure for precise wavelength control. Fabry-Perot lasers produce a broad emitting beam. Broad Area Laser Diodes have a large emitting aperture and are commonly used for high-power applications.</p></p>
