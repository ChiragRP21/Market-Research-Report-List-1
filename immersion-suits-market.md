<p><ul><li>Insulated Immersion Suits</li><li>Un-Insulated Immersion Suits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The immersion suits market can be broadly categorized into two types: insulated immersion suits and un-insulated immersion suits. Insulated immersion suits are designed to provide enhanced thermal protection in cold water conditions. They are equipped with insulation materials that prevent heat loss and promote survival in freezing temperatures. On the other hand, un-insulated immersion suits are primarily used in relatively warmer climates where thermal protection is not required. These suits offer buoyancy and protect the wearer from hypothermia, but do not provide additional insulation.</p></p>
