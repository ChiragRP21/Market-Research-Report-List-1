<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Automotive telematics systems are classified into three types based on their connectivity capabilities. Embedded type systems are built-in and directly installed in the vehicle, providing seamless connectivity and advanced functionalities. Tethered type systems rely on external devices like smartphones for connectivity and require manual intervention. Integrated type systems combine the features of embedded and tethered systems by using both built-in hardware and external devices. These systems offer enhanced connectivity, convenience, and a wide range of services to improve vehicle safety, efficiency, and entertainment options.</p></p>
