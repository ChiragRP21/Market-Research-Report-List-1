<p><p>The electro-mechanical brake market consists of different types such as single face brake, power off brake, particle brake, hysteresis power brake, and multiple disk brake. A single face brake is a simple brake system that uses friction to stop rotation. Power off brake engages when power is removed. Particle brake uses magnetic particles to control braking force. Hysteresis power brake also uses magnetic force but differs in operating principle. Multiple disk brake employs multiple disks to provide increased braking power. Each type has its own unique characteristics and applications in various industries.</p></p>
