They are commonly used in industries such as pharmaceuticals and petrochemicals. </p><p>On the other hand, solid acid-base catalysts are materials that possess acidic or basic properties and are in a solid state. They promote reactions by providing an active surface for reactants to interact with, thus accelerating the reaction rate. Solid acid-base catalysts find applications in various fields including catalytic converters and polymer synthesis.</p></p>
