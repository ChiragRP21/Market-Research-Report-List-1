<p><p>The outdoor forklift tire market comprises three main types: pneumatic forklift tires, solid forklift tires, and polyurethane forklift tires. Pneumatic forklift tires are filled with air and offer good traction and shock absorption, making them suitable for rough and uneven terrains. Solid forklift tires are made of solid rubber, providing puncture resistance and durability, but offering less cushioning. Polyurethane forklift tires are lightweight and non-marking, ideal for indoor use on flat and smooth surfaces. Each type has its own advantages and is preferred in different work environments.</p></p>
