<p><p>The Electric Power Steering System (EPS) market can be categorized into four types - Column-EPS (C-EPS), Pinion-EPS (P-EPS), Rack-EPS (R-EPS), and Dual Pinion-EPS (DP-EPS). C-EPS is mounted on the steering column and provides assistance to the driver. P-EPS is located at the end of the steering column, while R-EPS is integrated with the steering rack. DP-EPS utilizes two pinions to provide power assistance. These types differ in terms of their design and placement, but all aim to assist drivers in steering their vehicles more easily and efficiently.</p></p>
