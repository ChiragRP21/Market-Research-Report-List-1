<p><p>The Automobile Aluminium Alloy Plate Market includes two primary types: Cold Rolling and Hot Rolling. Cold Rolling involves passing the alloy plate through rollers at room temperature, resulting in plates with higher strength and smoother surface finishes. On the other hand, Hot Rolling involves heating the alloy plate to high temperatures and then passing it through rollers, yielding plates with better overall formability and lower operational costs. These two methods offer distinct advantages, making them suitable for various applications in the automobile industry.</p></p>
