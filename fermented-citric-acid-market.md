<p><p>The fermented citric acid market includes two major types: Citric Acid Anhydrous (CAA) and Citric Acid Monohydrate (CAM). CAA is a dry form of citric acid and is widely used in the food, beverage, and pharmaceutical industries. It is a white crystalline powder with high water solubility. On the other hand, CAM is citric acid combined with a single molecule of water. It is also used in various applications such as food preservation, flavor enhancement, and pharmaceutical formulations. Both types play a crucial role in the citric acid market, serving diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
