<p><p>Insulated metal panels (IMPs) consist of a rigid insulating foam core sandwiched between two metal panels. The market is divided into two types: insulated metal wall panels and insulated metal roof panels. Insulated metal wall panels are used for exterior walls of buildings, providing energy efficiency and improved thermal performance. On the other hand, insulated metal roof panels are specifically designed for roofing applications, offering durability, thermal insulation, and protection against weather elements. Both types of IMPs contribute to reducing energy consumption, enhancing building insulation, and improving overall sustainability.</p></p>
