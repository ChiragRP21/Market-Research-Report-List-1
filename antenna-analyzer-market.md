With the increasing adoption of wireless communication systems, the demand for antennas with better performance and efficiency is growing. Antenna analyzers play a crucial role in achieving these goals by providing accurate measurements and analysis, enabling engineers and technicians to optimize antenna designs.</p><p>Moreover, the development of new technologies, such as 5G networks and Internet of Things (IoT) devices, is expected to further drive the market growth.
