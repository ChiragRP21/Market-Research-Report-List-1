Heat stabilizer additives prevent degradation at high temperatures, and antioxidant additives delay polymer oxidation. Flame retardant additives inhibit or suppress the spread of flames in plastics, making them fire-resistant. Lastly, other additives include UV stabilizers, lubricants, antimicrobial agents, and biodegradable additives, among others, which offer additional benefits and functionalities to the plastics.</p></p>
