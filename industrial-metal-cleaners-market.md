<p><p>Industrial metal cleaners market includes various types of cleaners, but two prominent categories are alkaline cleaners and acid cleaners. Alkaline cleaners are formulated with a high pH, making them effective in removing oils, grease, and organic contaminants from metal surfaces. They are commonly used for general cleaning purposes. On the other hand, acid cleaners have a low pH and are specifically designed to remove scales, rust, and mineral deposits from metal surfaces. They are effective for descaling and restoration purposes in industrial applications. These two types of cleaners play a crucial role in maintaining the cleanliness and performance of metal surfaces in various industries.</p></p>
