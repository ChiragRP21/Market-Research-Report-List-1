<p><p>The GEO-Instruments market consists of two main types of devices: offline vibration monitoring devices and online vibration monitoring devices. Offline vibration monitoring devices are used to measure and analyze vibration levels in structures after an event or during maintenance operations. They do not provide real-time data but are useful for retrospective analysis. On the other hand, online vibration monitoring devices continuously monitor and transmit real-time data regarding vibration levels in structures. This enables immediate detection of any abnormal behavior, allowing for timely actions to prevent potential damage.</p></p>
