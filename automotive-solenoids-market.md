<p><strong>The Automotive Solenoids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2-Way Valve</li><li>3-Way Valve</li><li>4-Way Valve</li><li>5-Way Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive solenoids market includes various types of solenoid valves. A 2-way valve has two ports allowing flow in one direction and blocking it in the other. A 3-way valve has three ports directing flow between two different pathways. A 4-way valve contains four ports and controls the flow between two paths while maintaining a common connection. Similarly, a 5-way valve has five ports, diverting flow between two paths while keeping a common connection. These valve types find application in automotive systems for controlling fluid or air flow in a specific direction.</p></p>
