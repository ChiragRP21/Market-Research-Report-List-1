<p><p>Baby mattresses can be classified into two main types: foam mattresses and innerspring mattresses. Foam mattresses are made of high-density polyurethane foam and provide a firm and supportive surface for babies. They are lightweight, affordable, and easy to clean. On the other hand, innerspring mattresses have a core of metal springs covered with layers of padding and fabric. They offer a more traditional and bouncy feel and are generally more expensive than foam mattresses. Both types of mattresses are available in the market to cater to different preferences and budgets of parents.</p></p>
