<p><p>The electronic toothbrush market is divided into two primary types: vibration motion and rotation-oscillation motion. Vibration motion toothbrushes use rapid back-and-forth movements to effectively clean teeth and gums. They are generally more affordable and user-friendly. On the other hand, rotation-oscillation motion toothbrushes employ a rotating head that oscillates back and forth, reaching the gaps between teeth more efficiently. These toothbrushes often come with advanced features like pressure sensors and timers. Both types offer superior cleaning performance compared to manual toothbrushes, improving oral hygiene.</p></p>
