<p><p>DNA sequencing electrophoresis system is a technique used to determine the order or sequence of nucleotides in a DNA molecule. This technique is widely employed in various applications including genetic research, diagnostics, forensics, and personalized medicine. The process involves fragmenting the DNA molecule into smaller fragments, which are then separated according to their size using an electrical charge and a gel matrix. The separated fragments are then visualized using specific dyes or markers to interpret the DNA sequence.</p><p>The future outlook of the DNA sequencing electrophoresis system market looks promising.
