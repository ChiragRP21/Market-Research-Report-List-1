<p><p>The auto black box camera module market is categorized into two types: CCD and CMOS. CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) is a type of image sensor that converts light into electrical signals. CCD sensors typically provide high-quality images, low noise, and superior light sensitivity, making them suitable for applications where image quality is crucial. On the other hand, CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) sensors are known for their low power consumption, compact size, and faster image processing capabilities. CMOS sensors are often found in affordable camera modules used in devices like dashcams and smartphones.</p></p>
