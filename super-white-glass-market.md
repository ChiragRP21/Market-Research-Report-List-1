<p><p>Super white glass refers to a type of glass known for its high level of clarity and brightness. There are two main types of super white glass: rolled glass and float glass. Rolled glass is made by passing the molten glass through rollers to create a flat sheet. It is often used in architectural applications like windows and doors. Float glass, on the other hand, is made by pouring molten glass onto a bed of molten metal, resulting in a smooth and uniform sheet. Float glass is commonly used in automotive and electronics industries. Both types of glass contribute to the market for super white glass.</p></p>
