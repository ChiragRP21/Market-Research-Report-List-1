<p><p>Nickel powder is used as an electrode material in multi-layer ceramic capacitors (MLCC). The two main methods of producing nickel powder for this market are Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD). CVD involves the chemical reaction between gases to deposit nickel onto a substrate, while PVD involves the physical deposition of nickel atoms through evaporation or sputtering. Both methods result in the production of high-quality nickel powder that meets the specific requirements of MLCC electrode applications.</p></p>
