<p><p>Contact and contactless interface chip cards are smart cards that have an embedded chip and are used for various applications such as payment transactions, access control, ticketing, and identification. </p><p>Contact interface chip cards require physical contact with a card reader, where the chip is inserted into the reader's slot. The card is authenticated, and data is transferred between the card and the reader via electrical contacts on the card. Contactless interface chip cards, on the other hand, use near-field communication (NFC) technology to enable communication between the card and the reader without physical contact. The card is simply tapped or brought close to the reader for data transfer.</p><p>The contact and contactless interface chip card market has been witnessing significant growth in recent years.
