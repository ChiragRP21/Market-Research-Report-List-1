<p><p>Special and extruded graphite are types of graphite that have been processed and treated to enhance their properties for specific applications. Special graphite refers to graphite with high purity and fine grain size, which gives it exceptional thermal conductivity, electrical conductivity, and chemical resistance. It is commonly used in industries such as electronics, aerospace, and energy. Extruded graphite, on the other hand, is a form of special graphite that has been processed through extrusion, resulting in a texture and structure that is suitable for specific applications like electric discharge machining (EDM) electrodes and continuous casting molds.</p><p>The current outlook of the special and extruded graphite market is positive. The rapid growth of industries such as electronics, energy, and automotive is driving the demand for special graphite products.
