<p><p>PCM Paint, also known as Phase Change Material Paint, is a type of paint that incorporates phase change materials into its composition, enabling it to regulate temperature within a given space. PCM Paint is commonly used in buildings and in transportation vehicles to enhance energy efficiency and thermal comfort.</p><p>The future outlook for the PCM Paint market appears promising. The increasing demand for energy-efficient solutions in various industries, such as construction, automotive, and aerospace, is driving the growth of this market.
