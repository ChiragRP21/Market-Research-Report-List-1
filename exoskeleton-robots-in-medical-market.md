Upper body exoskeletons specifically target the upper limbs, providing support, strength, and stability to patients suffering from arm or hand disabilities. Full body exoskeletons encompass both lower and upper body functionalities, providing comprehensive assistance for patients requiring complete mobility support.</p></p>
