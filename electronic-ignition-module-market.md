<p><strong>The Electronic Ignition Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single coil</li><li>Multi-coil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic ignition module market is comprised of two main types: single coil and multi-coil systems. Single coil modules use a single ignition coil to generate spark for all the cylinders in an engine. On the other hand, multi-coil modules utilize multiple ignition coils, with each coil responsible for generating spark for a specific cylinder. Multi-coil systems offer better engine performance and efficiency as they can precisely control spark timing for each cylinder, while single coil systems are simpler and more cost-effective.</p></p>
