<p><ul><li>Chemical Control</li><li>Physical Control</li><li>Biological Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stored grain protectants market consists of three main types: chemical control, physical control, and biological control. Chemical control involves the use of synthetic insecticides or pesticides to kill or repel pests in stored grains. Physical control involves the use of equipment or techniques to prevent pests from accessing stored grains, such as airtight storage containers or proper cleaning practices. Biological control utilizes natural enemies of pests, such as predators or parasites, to control pest populations in stored grains. These different approaches offer various options for grain storage facilities to effectively protect their produce from pests.</p></p>
