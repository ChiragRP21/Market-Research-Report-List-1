<p><p>Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) Films market is categorized into several types, including Rigid PVC Films, Monomeric Flexible PVC Films, Polymeric Flexible PVC Films, and Others. Rigid PVC Films possess high rigidity and durability, making them suitable for applications like packaging and construction. Monomeric Flexible PVC Films are less durable but offer excellent printability, commonly used in advertising and labeling. Polymeric Flexible PVC Films are more durable and versatile, suitable for applications like automotive interiors and graphic films. The "Others" category includes specialty PVC films with unique properties like anti-fog, tamper-evident, or translucent films.</p></p>
