<p><p>A vapor canister, also known as an evaporative emission control system (EVAP) canister, is a component of a vehicle's emissions control system. It is designed to capture and store harmful fuel vapors from the fuel tank, preventing them from being released into the atmosphere. The canister is filled with activated charcoal, which absorbs the vapors and holds them until they can be burned off through the engine.</p><p>The future outlook for the vapor canister market is highly positive.
