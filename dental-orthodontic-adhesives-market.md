<p><strong>The Dental Orthodontic Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Dental orthodontic adhesives are classified into different types based on their curing mechanisms. Chemically-cured adhesives are activated by a chemical reaction, which causes them to harden. Light-cured adhesives rely on a light source, typically a dental curing light, to initiate the curing process. Dual-cured adhesives combine both chemical and light curing methods for increased flexibility. Other types of adhesives may include self-cured or heat-cured varieties. Each type has its own advantages and is used based on specific orthodontic treatment requirements.</p></p>
