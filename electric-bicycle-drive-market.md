<p><ul><li>Hub Motor</li><li>Center Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric bicycle drive market consists of two main types: Hub Motor and Center Motor. The Hub Motor system is integrated into the bike's wheels, providing direct power to them. It is known for its simplicity and ease of installation, making it a popular choice for DIY enthusiasts. On the other hand, the Center Motor system is located in the middle of the bike's frame, usually near the pedals. It offers a balanced weight distribution and a more natural riding experience, making it suitable for longer rides and hilly terrains.</p></p>
