<p><ul><li>Transverse-Output Gyrotron</li><li>Axial-Output Gyrotron</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Gyrotrons market comprises two main types: Transverse-Output Gyrotrons and Axial-Output Gyrotrons. Transverse-Output Gyrotrons produce microwave power by inducing an electron beam to move perpendicular to the magnetic field, generating high-frequency waves. They are primarily utilized in heating and plasma applications. On the other hand, Axial-Output Gyrotrons produce microwave power with electrons traveling parallel to the magnetic field axis, making them suitable for electron cyclotron resonance heating in fusion devices and research industries. Both types of Gyrotrons play a crucial role in various technological applications.</p></p>
