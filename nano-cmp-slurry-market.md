<p><p>Nano CMP slurry refers to a type of chemical mechanical planarization slurry used in the semiconductor industry for polishing and planarizing surfaces. Two common types of nano CMP slurries are colloidal silica slurries and ceria slurries. Colloidal silica slurries contain tiny silica particles suspended in a liquid medium and are known for their high removal rates and excellent planarization abilities. On the other hand, ceria slurries utilize cerium oxide particles and are preferred for applications that require a higher level of surface smoothness and defect removal.</p></p>
