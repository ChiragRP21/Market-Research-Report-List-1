<p><p>The steam turbine MRO market includes various types, namely axial, radial, mixed, tangential, and helical. Axial turbines consist of blades that rotate parallel to the shaft. Radial turbines have blades that rotate perpendicular to the shaft. Mixed turbines combine axial and radial flow characteristics. Tangential turbines have blades that wrap around the rotor in a spiral pattern. Helical turbines have blades that are twisted along the length of the rotor. Reentry market refers to the maintenance, repair, and overhaul services provided for steam turbines to ensure their efficient operation and prolong their lifespan.</p></p>
