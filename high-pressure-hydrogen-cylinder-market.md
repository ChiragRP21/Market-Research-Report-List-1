<p><p>The high pressure hydrogen cylinder market can be categorized into four types - Type I, Type II, Type III, and Type IV. Type I cylinders are made of steel and have a high weight and low storage capacity. Type II cylinders have a steel liner wrapped with carbon fiber, reducing weight and increasing storage capacity. Type III cylinders feature a composite liner wrapped with carbon fiber, offering further weight reduction and increased storage capacity. Lastly, Type IV cylinders utilize a plastic liner completely wrapped with carbon fiber, resulting in the lightest weight and maximum storage capacity.</p></p>
