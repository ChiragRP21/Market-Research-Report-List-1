<p><p>The automotive airbag parts market comprises four main types of components: airbag cushion, airbag flexible fabric bag, airbag inflation module, and airbag impact sensor. The airbag cushion is the part that inflates during a collision to protect the occupants. The airbag flexible fabric bag holds the cushion and provides a barrier between the cushion and the occupant. The airbag inflation module is responsible for inflating the cushion rapidly using a propellant. Lastly, the airbag impact sensor detects sudden deceleration or impact and triggers the inflation module to activate the airbag system.</p></p>
