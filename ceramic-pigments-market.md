<p><p>The Ceramic Pigments Market is categorized into two types: Simple Compound Type and Solid Solution-Oxide Type. Simple Compound Type refers to pigments made by mixing different compounds together. These pigments are known for their wide color range and high stability. On the other hand, Solid Solution-Oxide Type pigments are produced by dissolving metal oxide compounds into a crystal lattice. These pigments provide vibrant and durable colors and are widely used in ceramics, building materials, and other industries. Both types play a crucial role in the ceramic pigments market due to their unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>
