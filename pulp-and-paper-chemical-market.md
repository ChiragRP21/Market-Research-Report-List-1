<p><p>The pulp and paper chemical market comprises three types: functional chemicals, bleaching chemicals, and process chemicals. Functional chemicals enhance the performance of the final paper product with properties like sizing, binding, and coating. Bleaching chemicals are used to lighten the color and remove impurities from pulp. Process chemicals aid in maintaining the efficiency of various stages involved in paper production, such as pulping, washing, and drying. These chemicals play essential roles in enhancing the quality, efficiency, and sustainability of the pulp and paper industry.</p></p>
