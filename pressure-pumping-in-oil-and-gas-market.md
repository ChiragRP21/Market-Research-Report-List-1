<p><p>Pressure pumping in the oil and gas industry refers to the process of injecting fluids at high pressure into the wellbore to create fractures in the rock formations, allowing for the extraction of oil and gas. The increasing demand for oil and gas, coupled with the development of unconventional resources, is driving the demand for pressure pumping services. The market has also been positively impacted by technological advancements in drilling techniques and equipment.</p><p>Additionally, the need for wells to be hydraulically fractured multiple times to maintain or increase their production rates is further contributing to the growth of the pressure pumping market. This is especially true for mature oil and gas fields where production rates might decline over time.</p><p>Looking into the future, the pressure pumping market is expected to continue growing at a considerable rate.
