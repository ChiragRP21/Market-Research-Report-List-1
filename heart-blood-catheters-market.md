<p><p>Heart blood catheters are medical devices used to diagnose and treat various cardiovascular conditions. Angiography catheters are inserted into blood vessels to perform imaging tests. IVUS/OCT catheters use ultrasound or optical coherence tomography to examine vessel walls. Guiding catheters help guide other instruments to the desired location during procedures. Electrophysiology catheters are used in mapping and identifying abnormal heart rhythms. Balloon catheters are expanded within blood vessels to open blockages and improve blood flow. All these catheters cater to different aspects of cardiovascular diagnosis and treatment.</p></p>
