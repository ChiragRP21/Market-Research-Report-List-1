<p><strong>The Plastic Film Mulching Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compression Type</li><li>Embedded Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Plastic Film Mulching Machine market can be categorized into Compression Type and Embedded Type. Compression Type machines use rollers to press the plastic film onto the soil surface, creating a tightly sealed cover. This type of machine is generally more suitable for flat terrain. On the other hand, Embedded Type machines use blades to bury the plastic film into the soil, providing a smoother surface. These machines are often preferred for hilly or uneven terrains. Understanding the different types helps users choose the most appropriate machine for their specific needs.</p></p>
