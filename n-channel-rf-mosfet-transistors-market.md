On the other hand, GaN on Si transistors are fabricated by growing GaN directly on silicon substrates. Lastly, Si market refers to transistors made of silicon only. These different types offer varied performance characteristics, such as power handling, efficiency, and frequency range, catering to diverse requirements and applications in the RF electronics market.</p></p>
