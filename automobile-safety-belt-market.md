<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The automobile safety belt market is primarily categorized into two types: ELR Three Point Belt and ALR Three Point Belt. The ELR (Emergency Locking Retractor) Three Point Belt is designed to automatically lock in the event of sudden braking or impact, effectively restraining the occupant and minimizing the risk of injury. On the other hand, the ALR (Automatic Locking Retractor) Three Point Belt provides a locking feature that prevents any slack in the belt, ensuring a snug fit for the occupant throughout the journey. Both types of safety belts contribute to enhancing passenger safety and reducing the severity of injuries during accidents.</p></p>
