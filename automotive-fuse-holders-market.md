<p><p>Automotive fuse holders consist of two types, namely in-line fuse holders and panel mount fuse holders. In-line fuse holders are designed to be installed within the electrical wiring system, typically placed in-line with the circuit they protect. On the other hand, panel mount fuse holders are installed directly onto panels or surfaces, usually with screws or bolts. Both types secure automotive fuses and help protect the electrical circuits from damage caused by excessive current or short circuits.</p></p>
