<p><p>In the commercial jet engine market, two main types of turbines play crucial roles: low-pressure turbine (LPT) and high-pressure turbine (HPT). The LPT, located towards the rear of the engine, extracts energy from the exhaust gases and powers the engine's fan and compressors. Meanwhile, the HPT, positioned closer to the combustion chamber, extracts energy from the hot combustion gases to drive the engine's main shaft. Both turbines play integral roles in ensuring the engine's efficient performance and power generation.</p></p>
