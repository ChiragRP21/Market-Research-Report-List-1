<p><p>Microforce testers are devices used to measure the force required to actuate or move very small objects. They are widely used in industries like electronics, medical devices, and precision engineering. There are three main market types for these testers: semi-automatic, automatic, and manual. Semi-automatic testers require some human intervention and control to operate, combining manual and automated functions. Automatic testers, on the other hand, are fully automated and operate without any human intervention, providing precise and efficient testing. Lastly, manual testers rely solely on human operators to apply force manually and collect data, making them less efficient but more cost-effective for smaller-scale operations.</p></p>
