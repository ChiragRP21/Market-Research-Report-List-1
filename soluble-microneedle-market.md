Both types are designed to dissolve after insertion into the skin, delivering therapeutic substances directly into the body. These microneedles are used for various applications such as drug delivery, vaccination, and cosmetic treatments. They offer advantages like painless administration, precise drug release, and easy disposal, making them a promising technology in the medical and cosmetic industries.</p></p>
