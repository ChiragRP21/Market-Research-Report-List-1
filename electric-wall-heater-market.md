<p><p>Electric wall heaters are available in two main types: fixed and portable. Fixed wall heaters are permanently installed on a wall and are a popular choice for long-term heating solutions in residential and commercial buildings. They offer a more seamless appearance and can provide consistent warmth in specific areas. On the other hand, portable wall heaters are easy to move around and are designed for temporary or flexible heating needs.
