<p><p>Mosquito repellent and mosquito killer lamp market consists of various types of products aimed at repelling or eliminating mosquitos. Mats contain chemicals that repel mosquitos when heated. Aerosols are sprays that kill mosquitos upon contact. Creams are applied topically to repel mosquitos from the skin. Mosquito killer lamps attract and kill mosquitos using ultraviolet light, electrocution, or suction. These products cater to individuals seeking protection from mosquitos both indoors and outdoors.</p></p>
