<p><strong>The Coil Coating Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solventborne</li><li>Waterborne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coil coating additives refer to the substances used in the manufacturing process of coil coatings to enhance their performance. Solventborne coil coating additives are those that are dissolved in organic solvents to form a solution, whereas waterborne additives are dispersed in water. Solventborne additives offer high flexibility and compatibility, while waterborne additives are more environmentally friendly and offer lower volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions. Both types of additives are commonly used in the coil coating industry to improve the adhesion, corrosion resistance, and drying characteristics of the coatings.</p></p>
