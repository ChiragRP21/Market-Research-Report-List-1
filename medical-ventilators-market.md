<p><p>Medical ventilators are devices used to support patients with respiratory problems. The market for these devices can be categorized into two main types: single-use and reusable. Single-use ventilators are designed for one-time use only and are disposed of after each use. Reusable ventilators, on the other hand, can be sterilized and used for multiple patients.
