<p><p>Refinish paints market types include solvent-based coating, water-based coating, and powder coating options. Solvent-based coatings are made of synthetic solvents and offer durability and resistance to chemicals. Water-based coatings are environmentally friendly, as they are made of water and do not emit harmful fumes, but they may require multiple layers for full coverage. Powder coatings are solvent-free and are applied as a dry powder, which is then heated to form a protective layer known for its durability and resistance to corrosion.</p></p>
