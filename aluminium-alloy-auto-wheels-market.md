<p><p>The aluminium alloy auto wheels market can be categorized into three types: casting wheels, forging wheels, and other markets. Casting wheels are manufactured using the process of casting molten aluminium into a mold, which allows for intricate designs and cost-effectiveness. Forging wheels, on the other hand, involve shaping the metal through compression under high pressure, resulting in a stronger and more durable product. Lastly, the "other" market includes alternative wheel manufacturing methods such as spinning and flow forming, which offer unique benefits like lightweight construction and improved performance.</p></p>
