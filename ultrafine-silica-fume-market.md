<p><p>Ultrafine silica fume is a highly reactive material that is commonly used as a supplementary cementitious material in construction. There are three types of silica fume available in the market. Densified silica fume is the most commonly used type, as it has been processed to improve its flowability and handling properties. Semi-densified silica fume is partially processed and offers a balance between flowability and reactivity. Undensified silica fume is the raw form of the material and requires additional processing before it can be used effectively. Each type offers different benefits and is chosen based on specific project requirements.</p></p>
