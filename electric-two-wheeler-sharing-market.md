<p><p>The electric two-wheeler sharing market can be classified into two types: dockless and dock-based. In the dockless market, users can locate and unlock electric scooters or bikes through a smartphone app. These vehicles can be picked up and dropped off anywhere within a designated operating area, providing convenience and flexibility to users. On the other hand, in the dock-based market, electric scooters or bikes are parked and locked in designated docking stations. Users can pick up and drop off the vehicles at these stations, requiring them to follow a fixed route and returning the vehicle to a designated spot.</p></p>
