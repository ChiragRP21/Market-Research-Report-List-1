<p><p>The explosion-proof infrared cameras market is categorized into three types based on the wavelength of radiation they detect: shortwave, medium wave, and long wave. Shortwave cameras detect radiation with a wavelength ranging from 0.9 to 1.7 μm, while medium wave cameras detect radiation from 3 to 5 μm. Long wave cameras, on the other hand, detect radiation with a wavelength of 7 to 14 μm. Each type is specifically designed to operate in hazardous environments prone to explosions, ensuring safety and enabling visual monitoring in such high-risk areas.</p></p>
