<p><p>Power modules for electric vehicles (EVs) are essential components used in hybrid electric vehicles (HEVs), pure electric vehicles (EVs), and plug-in hybrid electric vehicles (PHEVs). These modules control the flow of electrical power between the vehicle's battery pack and the electric motor. In HEVs, power modules manage power distribution between the internal combustion engine and the electric motor. In EVs, power modules facilitate power transfer from the battery pack to the electric motor. Lastly, in PHEVs, power modules enable the efficient use of both the internal combustion engine and electric motor, offering a combination of gasoline and electric power.</p></p>
