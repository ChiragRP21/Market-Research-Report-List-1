The market for PAH treatment can be divided into different types, including Prostacyclin and Prostacyclin Analogs, SGC Stimulators, ERA, and PDE-5 inhibitors. Prostacyclin and Prostacyclin Analogs target the prostacyclin pathway, relaxing and widening blood vessels. SGC Stimulators stimulate the soluble guanylate cyclase enzyme, enhancing vasodilation. ERA (endothelin receptor antagonists) block endothelin receptors, reducing vessel constriction. PDE-5 inhibitors inhibit the PDE-5 enzyme, increasing nitric oxide levels and promoting vasodilation. These treatments aim to alleviate PAH symptoms and improve patients' quality of life.</p></p>
