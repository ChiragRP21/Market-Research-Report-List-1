<p><p>The Aerospace Carbon Fiber Fabric market consists of various types, namely 3k, 6k, 12k, and Others. These types refer to the number of carbon filaments contained per tow within a fabric. The 'k' unit represents a thousand filaments. A 3k fabric has 3,000 filaments per tow, a 6k has 6,000, and a 12k has 12,000. Other types beyond these three commonly used ones are referred to as "Others." The type of fabric chosen depends on specific aerospace applications, as each type offers different mechanical properties and performance characteristics.</p></p>
