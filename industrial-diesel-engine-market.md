<p><p>The industrial diesel engine market is categorized into two main types: two-stroke and four-stroke engines. Two-stroke engines complete the power cycle in just two strokes of the piston, making them simpler and easier to maintain but less efficient. Four-stroke engines, on the other hand, require four piston strokes to complete the power cycle, resulting in better fuel efficiency and lower emissions.
