<p><p>The tractor rear-view camera market consists of two main types: wired rear-view cameras and wireless rear-view cameras. Wired rear-view cameras require a physical connection between the camera and the tractor's display system, typically through cables. This ensures a reliable and uninterrupted transmission of video feed. On the other hand, wireless rear-view cameras transmit video wirelessly, eliminating the need for cables. They use technology like Wi-Fi or Bluetooth to establish a connection between the camera and the display system, providing flexibility and ease of installation.</p></p>
