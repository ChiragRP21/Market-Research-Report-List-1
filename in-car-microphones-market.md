<p><p>In-car microphones market can be categorized into two types: Bluetooth connection and wireless connection. </p><p>Bluetooth connection refers to the method of connecting the microphone to other devices in the car, such as the audio system or the driver's smartphone, using Bluetooth technology. This allows for convenient wireless communication and hands-free operation.</p><p>On the other hand, wireless connection refers to the technology used to transmit the audio signal from the microphone to the receiving device without the need for physical cables. This allows for more flexibility in microphone placement and eliminates the clutter of wires in the car.</p><p>Both Bluetooth and wireless connections provide convenience and freedom of movement, enhancing the overall driving experience.</p></p>
