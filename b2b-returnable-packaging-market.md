<p><p>B2B returnable packaging refers to packaging materials or containers that are used in business-to-business transactions and are designed to be returned to the vendor or supplier for reuse. <p><p>B2B Returnable Packaging refers to packaging solutions used by businesses for transporting and storing goods that can be reused multiple times, reducing waste. Two common types in this market are Intermediate Bulk Containers (IBCs) and Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers (FIBCs). IBCs are large, often collapsible containers used for transporting and storing liquid or bulk materials. FIBCs, on the other hand, are flexible bags made from woven polypropylene fabric used for transporting and storing dry or granular products. Both options offer cost-saving benefits and eco-friendly alternatives to traditional packaging methods.</p></p>
