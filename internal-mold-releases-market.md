<p><p>The Internal Mold Releases market can be categorized into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I refers to solvent-based internal mold releases that are primarily used in applications where the molded part requires a high-gloss finish. They provide excellent slip and release properties, allowing easy removal of the part from the mold. On the other hand, Type II internal mold releases are water-based and widely used in applications where a matte or textured finish is desired. They offer good mold release properties and are environmentally friendly.</p></p>
