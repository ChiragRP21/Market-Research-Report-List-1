<p><p>The adhesives TPU market is divided into four main types: water soluble TPU adhesive, water emulsion TPU adhesive, solvent-based TPU adhesive, and solid TPU adhesive. Water soluble TPU adhesive refers to adhesives that dissolve in water, making them easy to apply and remove. Water emulsion TPU adhesive is similar, but instead of dissolving completely, it forms an emulsion when mixed with water. Solvent-based TPU adhesive utilizes solvents to create a strong bond. Solid TPU adhesive comes in solid form and requires heat or pressure to activate the adhesive properties.</p></p>
