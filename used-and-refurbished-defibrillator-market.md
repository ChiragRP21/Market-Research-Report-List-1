<p><ul><li>Automatic External Defibrillators</li><li>Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators</li><li>Wearable Cardioverter Defibrillator</li><li>Advanced Life Support (ALS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The used and refurbished defibrillator market consists of various types of defibrillators. Automatic External Defibrillators (AEDs) are portable devices used by non-medical personnel to provide rapid treatment for sudden cardiac arrest. Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) are surgically implanted devices that continuously monitor and deliver electric shocks to restore normal heart rhythm. Wearable Cardioverter Defibrillators (WCDs) are worn externally and automatically detect and correct abnormal heart rhythms. Advanced Life Support (ALS) defibrillators are used by medical professionals in emergency situations, providing more advanced features and capabilities than other types.</p></p>
