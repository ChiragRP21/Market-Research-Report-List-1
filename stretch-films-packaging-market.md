<p><p>Stretch film packaging is comprised of different types of materials used in the market. Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) is a flexible and durable film suitable for a variety of products. Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) offers excellent stretchability and high clarity. Polyvinyl chloride (PVC) is commonly used for heavier or more rigid products. Polypropylene (PP) offers good tear resistance and is often used for packaging irregularly shaped items. Other materials used in stretch film packaging include high-density polyethylene (HDPE), polyester, and nylon. These materials provide different properties to meet specific packaging needs.</p></p>
