<p><p>The dental X-ray film processors market is categorized into two types: intraoral film processors and extraoral film processors. Intraoral film processors are used for developing dental X-ray films that are placed inside the patient's mouth. They are compact, easy to use, and provide high-quality image results. Extraoral film processors, on the other hand, are designed for developing dental X-ray films taken outside the patient's mouth, such as panoramic or cephalometric X-ray films. They are larger in size and offer specialized features for processing different types of X-ray films.</p></p>
