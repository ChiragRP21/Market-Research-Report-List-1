<p><ul><li>Short-wave Length Thermal Camera</li><li>Mid-wave Length Thermal Camera</li><li>Long-wave Length Thermal Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The security thermal cameras market consists of three types based on the wavelengths of the thermal sensors used. Short-wave length thermal cameras operate in the 1 to 3 μm range and are suitable for industrial and commercial applications. Mid-wave length thermal cameras function in the 3 to 5 μm range and are commonly used in military and surveillance systems. Long-wave length thermal cameras operate in the 8 to 14 μm range and are mainly utilized in firefighting, law enforcement, and medical imaging applications. These different types cater to various industries and applications based on their specific wavelength capabilities.</p></p>
