<p><strong>The Solar Cell Junction Boxes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Potting Solar Cell Junction Boxes</li><li>Non Potting Solar Cell Junction Boxes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solar cell junction boxes are an essential component of solar panel systems. Potting solar cell junction boxes are protected by encapsulation with materials like epoxy resin, enhancing their resistance to moisture, dust, and other environmental factors. On the other hand, non-potting solar cell junction boxes don't undergo the encapsulation process, making them less protected but potentially more cost-effective. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements of the solar panel system, such as the intended installation environment and budget constraints.</p></p>
