<p><p>Dental liners and bases are dental materials that are used to provide a protective layer between the tooth and restorative material. They are applied to the cavity preparation before placing the restorative material to protect the dental pulp, provide insulation, and stimulate dentin formation. Dental liners have a thin consistency, while dental bases have a thicker consistency and are used in deeper cavities.</p><p>The dental liners and bases market is witnessing significant growth due to various factors. First, the increasing prevalence of dental caries and other dental issues is driving the demand for these materials. Additionally, the rising awareness about the importance of oral health and the availability of advanced dental treatments are further fueling the market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, technological advancements in dentistry, such as the development of bioactive and adhesive materials, are contributing to market expansion.
