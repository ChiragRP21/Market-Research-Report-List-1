A multi-disc clutch uses multiple friction discs to transfer torque between the driving and driven shafts. A hydraulic coupling employs hydraulic fluid to transmit power between two shafts. An electromagnetic clutch utilizes an electromagnetic force to engage and disengage power transmission. These clutches find applications in various industries where precise torque transfer and smooth engagement are required, such as automotive, industrial machinery, and agriculture.</p></p>
