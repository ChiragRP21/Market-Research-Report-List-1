They are widely used in applications such as DNA sequencing, fuel cell research, and gas and liquid filtration.</p><p>3. Polyester Type: These filters are made of polyester material and offer good chemical resistance, low protein binding, and high flow rates. They find applications in water and environmental analysis, microbiological studies, and air pollution monitoring.</p></p>
