<p><p>Composite CNG cylinders are used for storing compressed natural gas (CNG) in vehicles. There are two main types: Type III and Type IV. Type III cylinders have a metal liner wrapped in a composite material, providing good durability and impact resistance. They are typically used for light-duty vehicles. Type IV cylinders, on the other hand, are fully composed of a composite material, offering a lightweight solution for vehicles. They are commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles and buses. Both types play a crucial role in the growing market for CNG cylinders.</p></p>
