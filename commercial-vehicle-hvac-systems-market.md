<p><p>Commercial Vehicle HVAC Systems refer to heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems installed in commercial vehicles. Automatic HVAC systems are equipped with sensors and controls that automatically adjust temperature, airflow, and humidity levels to maintain a comfortable environment. They offer ease of use and are widely preferred in modern vehicles. On the other hand, manual HVAC systems require manual adjustments by the vehicle occupants to control temperature and airflow. While they may lack the convenience of automatic systems, they are cost-effective and commonly found in older commercial vehicles.</p></p>
