<p><strong>The Disposable Correction Tape Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Paper-based</li><li>Polyester Film-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable correction tape market consists of two types: paper-based and polyester film-based. Paper-based correction tape utilizes a strip of paper coated with a dry correction fluid, allowing users to cover mistakes by gliding the tape over the error. Polyester film-based correction tape, on the other hand, uses a thin layer of polyester film for a smoother and more durable correction. Both types offer convenience by providing a quick and clean way to correct mistakes without smudging or leaving any residue on paper surfaces.</p></p>
