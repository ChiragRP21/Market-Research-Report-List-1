<p><p>The UV LED for Curing market can be divided into three types: UV-A LED, UV-B LED, and UV-C LED. UV-A LEDs emit longer wavelengths and are commonly used in applications such as counterfeit detection and nail curing. UV-B LEDs emit medium wavelengths, and are often used in applications like sterilization and phototherapy. UV-C LEDs emit shorter wavelengths and are highly effective in disinfection applications, such as water and air purification systems. These different types of UV LEDs cater to different industries and provide various benefits based on their wavelength ranges.</p></p>
