<p><p>Aqueous PU Dispersion market includes three types: Component Polyurethane, Two-component Polyurethane, and Urethane Modified. Component Polyurethane is a single-component system that does not require any additional mixing and is ready to use straight from the container. Two-component Polyurethane consists of two separate components that need to be mixed together before use. Urethane Modified refers to polyurethane dispersions that are modified with additional chemicals or additives to enhance certain properties such as adhesion or flexibility. These various types provide options for different applications and performance requirements in the market.</p></p>
