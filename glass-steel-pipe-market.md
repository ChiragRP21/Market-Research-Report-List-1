Epoxy glass steel pipes have an interior lining of epoxy resin, providing resistance to corrosion and chemicals. Polyester glass steel pipes have a polyester resin lining, offering good flexibility and electrical insulation. Vinyl Ester glass steel pipes have a vinyl ester resin lining, providing excellent resistance to high temperatures, corrosion, and chemicals. These types cater to different industrial needs.</p></p>
