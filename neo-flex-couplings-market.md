<p><p>Neo-Flex couplings are a type of flexible coupling used in various industries. Ribbed Neo-Flex couplings have a series of ridges or ribs on their surface, which helps in transmitting torque and providing flexibility. These couplings are commonly used in applications where there is a need for higher torque transmission and torsional flexibility. Smooth Neo-Flex couplings, on the other hand, have a smooth surface and are commonly used in applications where there is a need for low torque transmission and shaft misalignment compensation. Both types offer reliable and efficient power transmission solutions in different industrial settings.</p></p>
