<p><p>Dental Unit Waterlines (DUWL) are a crucial component of dental units that deliver water for various dental procedures. These waterlines can be categorized into two market types: Fixed Type and Mobile Type. Fixed Type DUWL are permanently installed in dental units and require professional maintenance for cleaning and disinfection. On the other hand, Mobile Type DUWL are portable and can be easily connected to dental units. They offer convenience and cost-effectiveness, but regular maintenance is still necessary to ensure water quality and prevent microbial contamination.</p></p>
