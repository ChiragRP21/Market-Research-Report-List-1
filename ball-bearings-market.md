<p><strong>The Ball Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Deep Groove Ball Bearing</li><li>Self-aligning Ball Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ball bearings market consists of two major types: deep groove ball bearings and self-aligning ball bearings. Deep groove ball bearings have a simple structure and are commonly used in various applications, such as electric motors and household appliances. Self-aligning ball bearings, on the other hand, have the ability to adjust themselves to accommodate misalignments between the shaft and the housing, making them ideal for applications with high misalignment or shaft deflection. Both types play a crucial role in enabling smooth and efficient rotary motion in diverse industries.</p></p>
