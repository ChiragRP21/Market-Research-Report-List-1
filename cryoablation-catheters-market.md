<p><strong>The Cryoablation Catheters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Point</li><li>Multielectrode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cryoablation catheters are devices used for treating abnormal tissue growth by freezing it. Single point cryoablation catheters have a single freezing tip, making them suitable for small or focal areas. These catheters are easier to control and maneuver. On the other hand, multielectrode cryoablation catheters have multiple freezing points, allowing for freezing larger areas or complex geometries. These catheters provide more flexibility in treating abnormal tissue growth, especially in cases where the target area is irregular or hard to reach.</p></p>
