<p><p>The Hermetic Sliding Door System market offers two types of doors: single leaf and double leaf. Single leaf doors consist of one panel that slides to open or close, creating a hermetically sealed entrance. These doors are often used for smaller spaces or areas with limited traffic flow. On the other hand, double leaf doors comprise two panels that slide simultaneously, providing a wider entryway. These doors are suitable for larger spaces or areas with higher traffic volumes, allowing for easier movement of people or equipment.</p></p>
