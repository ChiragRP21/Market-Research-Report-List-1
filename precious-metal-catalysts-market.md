<p><p>Precious metal catalysts are substances that facilitate and increase the rate of chemical reactions without being consumed themselves. The growing demand for cleaner and greener technologies, along with increasing regulations on emissions and pollution control, is driving the market. The rising demand for pharmaceutical products, coupled with the increasing complexity of drug synthesis, is expected to fuel the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the petrochemical industry is another significant consumer of precious metal catalysts, as they are used in the production of chemicals, plastics, and polymers. The development of alternative catalysts and technologies may also pose a threat to the market.</p><p>Overall, the precious metal catalysts market is projected to grow at a steady rate during the forecasted period, with a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 7.1%.
