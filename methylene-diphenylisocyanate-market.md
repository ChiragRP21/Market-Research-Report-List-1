<p><p>The Methylene Diphenylisocyanate (MDI) market consists of three main types: Polymeric MDI, Pure MDI, and Modified MDI. Polymeric MDI is a mixture of different isomers and is mainly used in the production of rigid foam insulation materials. Pure MDI is a single isomer and is commonly used in flexible foam applications. Modified MDI refers to the MDI blended with polyols or other additives for specific applications like coatings, adhesives, and sealants. These different types of MDI cater to various industry requirements and end-use applications.</p></p>
