The centrifugal type fan consists of blades that rotate in a radial direction, creating a higher pressure and airflow. The axial type fan comprises blades that rotate parallel to the airflow, generating a larger volume of air at a lower pressure. The "Others" category includes various types of fans that may use different mechanisms or technologies to extract smoke. These categories help differentiate and cater to different needs and preferences in the market for smoked extractor fans.</p></p>
