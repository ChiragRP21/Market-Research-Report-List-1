<p><p>A water-hammer arrestor is a device used to prevent the occurrence of water hammer, which is a sudden increase in pressure resulting from the sudden stop or change in flow of water within a plumbing system. Water hammer can cause damage to pipes, valves, and other plumbing components, leading to leaks and costly repairs. A water-hammer arrestor works by absorbing the shock waves caused by water hammer, thus minimizing the pressure surges and protecting the plumbing system.</p><p>The water-hammer arrestor market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period.
