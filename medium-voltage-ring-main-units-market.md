<p><p>The Medium Voltage Ring Main Units market consists of three types: Solid Insulated, Gas Insulated, and Air Insulated. Solid Insulated units use solid materials like polymeric insulation to provide protection and insulation for the system. Gas Insulated units use sulfur hexafluoride gas to insulate and extinguish arcs. Air Insulated units rely on air as the insulation medium and are typically used in outdoor installations. These three types cater to different needs and preferences in terms of insulation and safety in the Medium Voltage Ring Main Units market.</p></p>
