<p><strong>The Rubber Cementing Plug Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bottom Cementing Plug</li><li>Top Cementing Plug</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rubber cementing plug market consists of two main types: the bottom cementing plug and the top cementing plug. The bottom cementing plug is used to create a barrier at the bottom of the wellbore during cementing operations, preventing the displacement of drilling fluids. On the other hand, the top cementing plug is deployed at the top of the cement slurry to separate it from the drilling fluids. Both types of plugs ensure the integrity of the cementing process in oil and gas wells, making them crucial components in the industry.</p></p>
