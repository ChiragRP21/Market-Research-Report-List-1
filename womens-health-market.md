On the other hand, Nonhormonal Therapy refers to the use of alternative treatments, such as herbal supplements, lifestyle changes, or physical therapies, to manage menopausal symptoms without using hormonal medications. Nonhormonal Therapy provides women with additional options for symptom management during menopause.</p></p>
