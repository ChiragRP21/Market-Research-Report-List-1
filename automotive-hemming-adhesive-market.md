<p><p>The automotive hemming adhesive market is categorized into two types, namely one-component and two-component. One-component adhesives are pre-mixed and ready for use without any additional mixing or catalysts. They offer ease of application, reducing processing time and costs. On the other hand, two-component adhesives require the mixing of two different components, usually a resin and a hardener, before use. These adhesives provide higher strength and durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. However, they involve additional preparation and handling steps, increasing process complexity.</p></p>
