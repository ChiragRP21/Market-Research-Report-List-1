<p><p>Personal Health Record (PHR) software helps individuals maintain and manage their health information digitally. Two types of PHR software include web-based and cloud-based solutions. Web-based PHR software runs on the internet and requires a web browser to access. Users can log in to their accounts from any device with an internet connection. Cloud-based PHR software stores health data on remote servers rather than the user's device, allowing easy access and synchronization across multiple devices. Both types offer convenience in accessing and storing health information securely.</p></p>
