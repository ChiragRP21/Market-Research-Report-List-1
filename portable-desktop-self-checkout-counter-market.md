<p><ul><li>Fluorescence detection</li><li>Magnetic detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable desktop self-checkout counter market can be categorized into two types: fluorescence detection and magnetic detection. Fluorescence detection utilizes a fluorescent dye that is coated on the currency or product, which emits a specific wavelength of light when illuminated. This allows for easy identification and authentication of the item. On the other hand, magnetic detection relies on magnetic ink or strips embedded in the currency or product. The counter uses magnetic sensors to detect and verify the presence and characteristics of these magnetic features, ensuring the security and validity of the item.</p></p>
