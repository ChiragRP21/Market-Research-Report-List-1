<p><p>The medium voltage metal-enclosed switchgear market consists of three types: air insulated metal-enclosed switchgear, gas insulated metal-enclosed switchgear, and solid insulated metal-enclosed switchgear. Air insulated switchgear uses air as the insulation medium, while gas insulated switchgear uses SF6 gas to insulate the components. Solid insulated switchgear uses solid materials, such as epoxy resin or silicone rubber, for insulation. These types of switchgear are used in medium voltage applications to control and protect electrical equipment in industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
