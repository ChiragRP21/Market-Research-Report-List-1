<p><p>The automotive market for composite CNG tanks consists of two main types: glass fiber composites and carbon fiber composites. Glass fiber composites are made by infusing glass fibers with a polymer resin, resulting in a lightweight and durable material suitable for CNG tanks. Carbon fiber composites, on the other hand, are produced by combining carbon fibers with a polymer matrix, offering even higher strength-to-weight ratio than glass fiber composites. Both types of composites are commonly used in the manufacturing of CNG tanks for automobiles, providing a safer and more efficient fuel storage solution.</p></p>
