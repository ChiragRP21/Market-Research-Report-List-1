<p><strong>The Grouting Additive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-Component</li><li>Two-Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Grouting additives are divided into two types: One-Component and Two-Component. One-Component grouting additives are premixed and ready to use upon purchase. They require no additional components or mixing on-site, making them convenient and time-saving. Two-Component grouting additives, on the other hand, come in two separate components that need to be mixed together before use. This type offers greater flexibility for customization and allows for adjustments in the ratio of components, ensuring optimal performance for various grouting applications.</p></p>
