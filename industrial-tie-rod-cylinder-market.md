<p><p>The Industrial Tie Rod Cylinder market comprises two main types: Hydraulic Cylinders and Pneumatic Cylinders. Hydraulic Cylinders use hydraulic fluid to generate power, providing heavy-duty and high-pressure performance suitable for applications like construction equipment and industrial machinery. On the other hand, Pneumatic Cylinders utilize compressed air or gas to exert force and are commonly used in lighter applications such as automation systems and robotics. Both types offer various sizes, configurations, and specifications, catering to diverse industrial needs for linear motion and force generation.</p></p>
