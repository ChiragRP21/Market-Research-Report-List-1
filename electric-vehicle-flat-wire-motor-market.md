<p><p>The electric vehicle flat wire motor market consists of three main types: hairpin flat wire motor, wave winding flat wire motor, and centrally winding flat wire motor. This classification is based on the design and structure of the motor's flat wires. Hairpin flat wire motors have a unique shape that allows for better cooling and higher power density. Wave winding flat wire motors use a wave-shaped winding pattern, which improves torque output and reduces losses. Centrally winding flat wire motors have a centrally positioned winding structure, enabling better heat dissipation and compactness.</p></p>
