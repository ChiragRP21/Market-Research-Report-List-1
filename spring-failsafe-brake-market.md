<p><ul><li>Dualspring Brake</li><li>Monospring Brake</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Spring Failsafe Brake market can be categorized into two types - Dualspring Brake and Monospring Brake. The Dualspring Brake is equipped with two springs that provide redundancy in case one fails, ensuring a failsafe operation. On the other hand, the Monospring Brake features a single spring mechanism for absorbing and distributing braking forces. The market for both types caters to various industries where reliable and efficient failsafe braking systems are required, such as automotive, industrial, and heavy machinery sectors.</p></p>
